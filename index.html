FAQ

What you should know about our website designs.
What format do the Figma designs come?
All our website designs come in the PNG and SVG format. Fully editable for customization.
What does Live updates mean?
Opting for the Business package grants access to Figma files for up to three team members, along with the added advantage of receiving new website designs added on a monthly basis.
Are the Icons & logos free to use?
Icons found in our designs are free to use and adapt accordingly to your design preferences. Every logo that is used in our website designs, is copy-right free and can be used for non commercial purposes.
Is it a one-time payment?
Our plans include one time payment. Meaning, there are no recurring charges.
Will I get all font styles for my designs?
Every font style that is used in our website design templates, comes packed with the Figma file.
Are the mockups copy-right free?
Mockups used in our website designs are designed and generated from our team of designer, hence they are copy-right free, and you can reuse freely.
